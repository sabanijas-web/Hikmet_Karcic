Invited lectures at universities

  • Trinity University, San Antonio, Texas, USA – “The roots of the genocide in Bosnia-Herzegovina, 1992–1995” Invited lecture, Departments of Political Science and International Studies · 3 November 2025
    Dr. Hikmet Karčić was hosted by Trinity University to discuss the historical and structural roots of the Bosnian genocide with students and faculty.
  • Manhattan College, New York, USA – “Roots of the Bosnian Genocide” Public lecture, School of Liberal Arts · January 2026
    At Manhattan College, Dr. Karčić addressed the audience on the ideological and institutional groundwork that led to the genocide in Bosnia and Herzegovina.
  • Keene State College, Keene, New Hampshire, USA – Guest lectures on Holocaust & genocide studies Guest teaching / workshops, Cohen Center for Holocaust and Genocide Studies · 2017–present
    As part of a fellowship, Dr. Karčić delivered multiple guest lectures to American students on the Bosnian war, concentration camps, and comparative genocide studies.
  • University of the Arts London (UAL), London, UK – “The Roots of the Bosnian Genocide” Introductory plenary lecture, international conference “Why Remember?” · 8 July 2023
    As keynote speaker, Dr. Karčić framed the discussion on memory, trauma, and education in the context of the Bosnian genocide.
  • University of Vienna, Vienna, Austria – “Deep Consequences of the Genocide in Bosnia and Herzegovina” Panel discussion / book promotion, Vienna Academy of Education · 31 October 2023
    As part of the “BÖJ” project, Dr. Karčić presented his book Torture, Humiliate, Kill and discussed the long‑term societal consequences of the genocide.
  • University of Regensburg, Regensburg, Germany – guest lectures on genocide and war crimes Invited teaching and research collaboration · 20XX–present
    Dr. Karčić has been a guest researcher at the University of Regensburg, delivering lectures on the Bosnian war, war crimes trials, and comparative genocide studies.

International conferences & keynote talks

  • International Association of Genocide Scholars (IAGS), University of Barcelona, Spain Paper presentation: “Trials during wartime: War crimes cases during the Bosnian war 1992–1995” · 10–14 July 2023
    At the 16th IAGS conference “Authoritarianism & Genocide: Narratives of Exclusion”, Dr. Karčić presented a paper on the conduct of war crimes trials during the conflict.
  • Memorial Center Kigali, Rwanda – “Genocide in Srebrenica 1995” International scientific conference “Ethnicity and genocide: Guidelines for prevention” · 8–9 November 2024
    Dr. Karčić spoke on the genocide in Srebrenica and its connections to denial and glorification of crimes against Bosniaks.
  • Centre for International Criminal Justice, Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam, Netherlands Paper presentation: “Challenges of Integrating Accounts of the Traumatized” · 29–30 April 2025
    At the conference “Yugoslav Wars and Post‑Yugoslav Space: Thirty Years After”, Dr. Karčić analyzed how testimonial narratives of survivors are integrated into academic and legal narratives.
  • Harriman Institute, Columbia University, New York, USA Book talk: “Torture, Humiliate, Kill: Inside the Bosnian Serb Camp System” · 2022
    Dr. Karčić presented his monograph in a public book talk hosted by the Harriman Institute, discussing the camp system as a mechanism of collective traumatization.
  • Sarajevo Security Conference (years of active participation) Panel discussions on genocide, denial, and security implications
    As director and participant, Dr. Karčić has contributed to multiple panels on the Srebrenica genocide, denial, and regional security challenges.

For the most up‑to‑date list of lectures and conferences, see Dr. Karčić’s profile at the Institute for Research of Crimes against Humanity and International Law, University of Sarajevo.
